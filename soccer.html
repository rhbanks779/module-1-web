YSOC's Mission

BCRL aims to serve the community of Batavia. We offer baseball, softball, soccer, basketball and now volleyball. We are a federal 501c3 organization fully funded by the registration fees.

No official affiliation with Batavia schools and certainly none of your village or township taxes support BCRL. With that we have to be wise with our funds and donations are always welcome, no matter how small.
